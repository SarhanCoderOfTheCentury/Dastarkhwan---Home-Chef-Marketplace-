DastarKhwan — Home Chef Marketplace
A commission-based platform connecting Karachi's home cooks with nearby households seeking authentic, trusted, weekly meal subscriptions and catering. Built for real pain, demo-able in 2 minutes.

No food trust
Restaurant food in Karachi is inconsistent — oil quality, hygiene, and ingredient freshness are unknown to the buyer. People don't trust what they're eating.
Chef profiles with verified kitchen photos, ingredient sourcing info, and real reviews from neighbours.
Home cooks are invisible
Thousands of skilled home cooks (especially women) have no channel to monetise. They rely on WhatsApp word-of-mouth with no scalable reach.
A chef onboarding flow that goes live in under 10 minutes — menu, pricing, availability, delivery radius.
Daily ordering fatigue
Families don't want to think about lunch every day. The mental load of deciding, ordering, and tracking is exhausting — especially for working households.
Weekly meal subscription with auto-delivery and a pause/skip feature — zero daily decisions.
Catering is chaotic
Booking a home cook for a dawat or office event involves 3+ WhatsApp threads, upfront cash, zero accountability, and frequent last-minute cancellations.
Structured catering bookings with upfront quotes, escrow payment, and calendar confirmation.
Core MVP features + wow differentiators that judges will remember.
Hyper-local discovery
Browse chefs within your neighbourhood or 3km radius. See their home area, delivery days, and signature dishes at a glance.
Weekly meal subscriptions
Subscribe to a chef for 5 or 7 days per week. Pause, skip, or swap before the weekly cutoff. Auto-renews until cancelled.
WOW
AI meal planner
User inputs dietary goals (low-carb, high-protein, baby-friendly). AI matches them to nearby chefs whose menus fit — personalised weekly suggestions.
WOW
Kitchen transparency feed
Chefs post short cooking clips to their profile — a TikTok-style hygiene reel. Subscribers see today's prep in real time before their delivery.
WOW
Community group orders
Neighbours in the same building or street pool a single order to unlock a lower price. Chef gets a larger batch — everyone wins.
Catering booking
Request a chef for your dawat with guest count, menu preferences, and date. Chef sends a quote within 2 hours. Escrow payment on confirmation.
Manual Delivery Coordination
Chef manually assigns external rider details (name & phone) and updates the order status, triggering instant customer updates and WhatsApp alerts.
WOW
Chef trust score
Algorithmic score combining hygiene photo verification, on-time delivery rate, order completion %, and verified review count. Shown prominently on each chef card.

30-second pitch
"In Karachi, millions of skilled home cooks can't reach customers beyond WhatsApp, while families order from restaurants they don't trust. DastarKhwan fixes both sides. We don't use GPS tracking or a rider network — home chef delivery is personal and coordinated externally. When Chef Sana assigns a rider and marks your order out for delivery, you get a WhatsApp message with the rider's name and phone number. That's how it actually works in this context, and customers trust it more than a moving pin. We take 15% per order, and our AI matches your dietary goals to the right chef nearby. We're not disrupting food delivery — we're creating a new category: the neighbourhood kitchen economy."
